Why This Project?

Liver cirrhosis is a serious health condition that leads to scarring of the liver and loss of liver function over time. Early diagnosis is crucial.

This project uses machine learning techniques to predict the likelihood of cirrhosis based on patient data. Our model was trained using clinical data, and the prediction interface is built using Flask and TensorFlow.

The goal is to assist healthcare professionals and individuals with a quick, data-driven risk assessment.
